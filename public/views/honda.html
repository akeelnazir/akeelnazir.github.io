CR-V Top Features

Turns heads
with every turn

Confident, bold, assertive. Words that not only describe the CR-V's personality, but its looks too.

Take its deep, sculpted body lines - they're what make its striking, desirable shape stand out on the road. And with its aerodynamic design you'll find visits to the fuel station become noticeably less frequent.

But it's not just the outside that looks good. Step inside and you'll find a luxury interior surrounded by wide, expansive windows for a spacious feel. It doesn't matter if you're in the driving seat, or watching it pass by - you won't be able to take your eyes off it.

Outstanding
on the inside

Decisions can be hard to make. So we've kept them to a minimum and made both of our engines fuel efficient, powerful and practical. Now it's just down to preference - petrol, or diesel.

  • {{item.title}}

The 155PS 2.0 i-VTEC petrol engine features Variable Valve Timing and Lift Control. This gives optimised output at high engine rpm, and fuel efficiency at lower rpm. It's available with a 5-speed automatic or 6-speed manual transmission, in either 2WD or 4WD.

An impressive 350NM of torque is available from the all-aluminium, 150PS 2.2 i-DTEC diesel engine, ideal for those looking to tow. The engine is matched to a 5-speed automatic or 6-speed manual transmission and comes with 4WD.

The latest technology
all within reach

Driving doesn't need distractions. That's why we've carefully positioned the latest technology right at your fingertips.

Take our steering wheel. You hold the power to change the music, check your fuel, or get directions* - all at the touch of button.

And for hands-on tasks like reversing, the CR-V will help you all the way, with a tilt of the passenger wing mirror so you can see the curb, and a clever rear view camera** to show where you're heading. You won't have to worry about a thing.

  • *Sat Nav is optional
  • **Standard on SE grade upwards

Eco Assist

Everyone wants to be a better driver. And with Eco Assist, you'll soon be on your way to doing so. Just keep an eye on the ambient meter around the speedometer - it changes colour depending on how you drive, which helps you achieve greater fuel efficiency on every journey. Handy, don't you think?

ECON Button

Push the button. Go on, push it. If you do you'll achieve maximum fuel efficiency while you drive. When activated, fuel economy improves by adjusting the performance of other things, like air con, cruise control and even your engine transmission*.

  • *Engine transmission adjusts on i-VTEC models only

i-MID

Intelligent Multi-Information Display. Or i-MID as we like to say. The i-MID screen sits in front of you at eye level showing you directions, your fuel consumption and even your favourite music to pick from - all controlled via the steering wheel.

Instinctive controls

Entertainment at the push of a button. And stats. And directions too. You'll find the buttons positioned exactly where your fingertips naturally sit on the steering wheel, so you can have total control without taking your eyes off the road.

Satellite Navigation

Destination - home. Just one of the many things you can say to the optional Voice Recognition Satellite Navigation system*. It will get you to your destination quickly and efficiently, while turn-by-turn directions are conveniently shown on the i-MID screen.

  • *Voice recognition only available on EX and SR grades

Room
to spare

Whether you're taking the kids on holiday or going on your own adventure, you'll find plenty of space in the back.

Open the tailgate at the touch of a button* and you'll see we've increased the boot space in our new CR-V by 147 litres. That's enough to hold three big suitcases. A chest of drawers. Or even a Great Dane. But, if you need even more room, our handy fold-flat seats drop smoothly at the pull of a lever.

So no matter where you're thinking of going, the CR-V has more than enough room to accommodate you.

  • *Available on EX grade only

Keeps you safe
in hazardous situations

Sometimes driving conditions will test you. But our CR-V is ready for whatever the road brings.

Take the Vehicle Stability Assist - it monitors all four wheels, making sure they have the right amount of power and braking if traction is lost. While the electronic 4WD works with the Motion Adaptive Electronic Power Steering to aid control - great for when the weather takes a turn for the worse.

Then there are our extensive safety options like the Advanced Driver Assist System. This includes Collision Mitigation Braking, Lane Keeping Assist and Adaptive Cruise control. By scanning the road ahead, the CR-V applies the brakes and corrects steering when needed.

It's probably why the new CR-V has received the 5-Star Euro NCAP Overall Safety Rating - that's the highest award possible.

Stay out on the road
and out of the petrol station

Journeys shouldn't be stop-start. That's why we introduced our ECON button. When pressed, the new CR-V modifies its systems to deliver excellent fuel economy and some of the lowest CO2 emissions in its class from just 149 g/km*.

The ambient meter changes colour based on your driving, showing you how to get the most from every tank of fuel and stay on the road for longer. So, switch it on, sit back and enjoy the ride.

  • *Emission figures shown are for the 2.2l i-DTEC S & SE grades with manual transmission and 4WD
